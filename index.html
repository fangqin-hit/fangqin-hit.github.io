Dr. Qin Fang

Researcher in Soft Robotics, Bionic Robots, and Medical Robots

About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Information Science and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology, Weihai. My research focuses on soft actuators, bionic robots, and micromanipulation robots.

  • M.S. in Aerospace Engineering, Zhejiang University, 2018–2021
  • Bachelor's in Mechatronics, Zhejiang University of Technology, 2014–2018
